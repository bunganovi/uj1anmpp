About Switzerland

Switzerland is a small country located in Central Europe, known for its natural beauty such as the Alps,
clear lakes and charming villages. The capital is Bern, while other major cities include Zurich,
Geneva and Lucerne.

Experience the Magic of Switzerland

Switzerland is not just about the beauty in sight, but also about the tranquillity in feel.
Behind the mist of the Alps and the ripples of Lake Lucerne lie stories of
peace that can only be understood by those who come to experience it.

Unique Facts Switzerland

Switzerland is the only country in the world that has enough bunderground shelter (bunker)
capacity for its entire population.This is part of a civil defence
policy that dates back to the Cold War era.

A Peaceful Capital in the Heart of Historical Switzerland

Bern, the capital of Switzerland, is a city that perfectly balances historical elegance and modern order. UNESCO World Heritage-listed Bern's old town is a beautiful maze of medieval architecture, filled with arcades, sculptural fountains and small shops.

The Land of Cheese Frozen in Time

Gruyères is a small town in western Switzerland that captivates with its medieval charm. Widely recognised as the home of the world-famous Gruyère cheese, the town offers an authentic experience for anyone looking to experience the tranquil and picturesque atmosphere of the classic countryside.

Road rules in switzerland
